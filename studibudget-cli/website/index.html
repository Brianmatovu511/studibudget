studibudget

Track income, expenses, and tuition runway — right from your terminal.

Created by Brian Doctor

Downloads

Why studibudget?

Students juggle classes, mini-jobs, and bills. studibudget helps you see your balance fast and know exactly how many months of tuition you can cover.

Install

cargo install --git https://github.com/your-username/studibudget

Or download a prebuilt static Linux binary below.

Usage

studibudget add income 450 "Kaufland shift"
studibudget add expense 320 "Rent part"
studibudget summary --last 20
studibudget runway 250
studibudget export out.csv

Who is it for?

International students in Germany who need simple budgeting and tuition planning in seconds.
